Our AI data safety rules (skill A4)
  1. Always use a company-approved AI tool.
  2. Never share sensitive personal information with any AI.
  3. Only share company information with our approved enterprise tools — Microsoft Copilot (Basic / 365), ChatGPT Enterprise, or Claude Team. Never with free or personal AI accounts.
  4. Where you can, anonymise the data first (take out names, prices, counterparties).
  5. AI sometimes “hallucinates” — it makes things up. Always check anything important. Don’t trust it blindly.
