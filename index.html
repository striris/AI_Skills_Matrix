Our AI data safety rules (skill A1)
  1. Always use a company-approved AI tool.
  2. Never share sensitive personal information with any AI.
  3. Only share company information with our approved enterprise tools — Microsoft Copilot (Basic / 365), ChatGPT Enterprise, or Claude Team. Never with free or personal AI accounts.
  4. AI sometimes “hallucinates” — it makes things up. Always check anything important. Don’t trust it blindly.

B

Which tool for which task

For each task, find the best starting tool. ★ = best choice · ✓ = works well · — = not suited. Many tasks have more than one good option — pick what you already have access to.

Heads-up on Claude + MicrosoftIn our environment Claude can’t currently connect to Microsoft 365 — no access to Outlook email, Teams or SharePoint. For inbox, Teams and SharePoint jobs, use Copilot or Power Automate. Claude still works fully on files you upload and on non-Microsoft connectors.

Rule of thumb

  • Working inside an Office app (Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, Word)? → Copilot 365.
  • Big PDF / contract, deep analysis, or build a live tool? → Claude.
  • Quick draft, rewrite or summary — or you only have basic Copilot? → Copilot Basic.
  • Repetitive file / email routing on a schedule? → Power Automate.
  • Coding or ad-hoc number-crunching? → Claude or ChatGPT.
